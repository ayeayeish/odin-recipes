Description

This is a recipe inspired by Asian cuisine. It has a nice balance of protein, and veges. Serve with rice. Enjoy.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • yoghurt

Spice Ingredients

  • Cumin Seeds
  • Coriander Powder
  • Chili Powder
  • Salt
  • Turmeric

Steps

  1. Fry Onions until caramelised - set aside
  2. Fry Ginger and Garlic Until fragrant
  3. Add chopped tomatoes - cook with lid on until soft
  4. Add spices - cook for 30 secs
  5. Add the onions - then the chopped chicken- stir - cook for 1 min, then cover with lid and continue cooking
  6. Once chicken is cooked, add yoghurt and cook for further couple of mins
  7. Serve up with rice!
