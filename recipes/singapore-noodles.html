Singapore Fried Noodles

Description

This is a recipe inspired by Asian cuisine. It has a nice balance of protein, carbs, and veges. Enjoy.

Ingredients

'Hard' Ingredients

  • Rice Vermicelli
  • Char siu Pork
  • Prawns
  • Capsicums
  • Onions

'Wet' Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Curry Powder
  • White pepper powder
  • Shaoxing Cooking Wine

Steps

  1. Soak the Rice Vermicelli
  2. Mix the 'Wet' Ingredients
  3. Fry the onions with the capsicum until charred
  4. Fry the prawns
  5. Toss in the soaked noodles and stir
  6. Add the wet ingredient mixture and stirfry
  7. Serve up!
