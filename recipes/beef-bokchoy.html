Beef and Bok Choy

I have no claims to this image and do not profit from this image

Description

This is a recipe inspired by Asian cuisine. It has a nice balance of protein, and veges. Serve with rice. Enjoy.

Ingredients

'Hard' Ingredients

  • Thin Sliced Beef
  • Bok Choy
  • Garlic
  • Ginger

'Wet' Ingredients

  • Dark Soy Sauce (4 Tbsp)
  • Shaoxing Wine (2 Tbsp)
  • Mirin (6 Tbsp)
  • Sesame Oil (2 Tsp)

Steps

  1. Fry Ginger and Garlic until fragrant
  2. Add beef and fry until browned
  3. Chop up bok choy, or leave whole, put this into the wok or pan
  4. Pour in the wet ingrdients, and add about half a cup of water
  5. Cook this with the lid on until bok choy becomes soft and beef is cooked
  6. Serve up!
